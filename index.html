What Is A Graphic Card?

A graphics card provides high-quality visual display.
by processing and executing graphical data.

What Is Real Time Ray Tracing

ray tracing is a rendering technique for generating an image.
by tracing the path of light as pixels in an image plane
and simulating the effects of its encounters with virtual objects.
